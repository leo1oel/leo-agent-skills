# Research taste

Use this skill when Leo's research taste would improve the answer.

## Workflow

1. Read `references/TASTES.md`.
2. Select the focused module that matches the task.
3. For idea generation, problem selection, critique, predictions, or research strategy, read `references/tastes/problem-selection.md`.
4. For experiment design, evaluation, engineering rigor, debugging, or research artifacts, read `references/tastes/method-and-rigor.md`.
5. Treat Leo's taste as guidance, not evidence.
6. Push back when the evidence disagrees with the preference.

## Default stance

Prefer research that clarifies mechanisms, exposes bottlenecks, creates useful abstractions, or rules out plausible wrong directions.
Be skeptical of novelty theater, clean narratives without evidence, and expensive plans that cannot be falsified quickly.
